Services

Advisory for the Business of Art

Investment-banking discipline and collection expertise, applied to the particular needs of galleries, collectors, and cultural enterprises. Every engagement is independent, confidential, and built around your objectives.

I

Art M&A Advisory

Strategic guidance for galleries, auction businesses, and art-adjacent companies through mergers, acquisitions, partnerships, and successions.

  • Sell-side and buy-side advisory
  • Valuation and deal structuring
  • Partnership and succession planning
II

Capital Raising

Capital formation tailored to art enterprises, with structures designed around the realities of the art business.

  • Private placements
  • Debt and growth financing
  • Equity offerings
III

Gallery & Cultural Finance

Financial solutions for expansion, exhibitions, and cultural infrastructure, drawing on private and institutional capital sources.

  • Expansion and build-out financing
  • Exhibition and program funding
  • Capital planning
IV

Collection Advisory

Sophisticated support for building, holding, and transitioning serious collections.

  • Acquisition and disposition strategy
  • Valuation analysis
  • Estate and legacy planning
V

Art-Secured Lending

Access to liquidity against fine-art collateral, structured to preserve ownership and optionality.

  • Lender introductions and structuring
  • Collateral and term analysis
  • Liquidity planning
VI

Collector Wealth Strategy

Long-term strategy for collectors who hold art as a meaningful asset class.

  • Portfolio diversification
  • Succession and generational transfer
  • Coordination with existing advisors
