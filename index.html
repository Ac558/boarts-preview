About Bo Arts LLC

Custodians of Cultural Continuity

The Bank of Arts is the advisory practice of Bo Arts LLC, an independent firm dedicated to the business of art. We exist because the art world and the financial world rarely speak the same language — and the cost of that gap falls on galleries, collectors, and the institutions that preserve our shared cultural legacy.

We bring financial discipline to cultural assets while honoring what makes them more than assets. Every engagement is led personally by a principal, held in confidence, and measured by your outcomes.

Engagements are led by senior advisory principals with finance, art-market, and transaction experience.

Frequently Asked

Clarity, Up Front

Are you a bank?

No. The Bank of Arts is a brand of Bo Arts LLC, an independent advisory firm. We are not a bank or depository institution, and we do not accept deposits. The name reflects our discipline and discretion, not a banking license.

Are you registered or regulated?

Investment advisory and securities services, where applicable, are provided through affiliated registered advisers and broker-dealer partners (FINRA/SIPC). General advisory and strategic services are provided directly by Bo Arts LLC. We are glad to clarify which capacity applies to your engagement.

Who do you typically work with?

Galleries and dealers, private collectors and family offices, and cultural enterprises — generally where art and meaningful capital intersect.

How do engagements begin?

With a confidential conversation. From there we propose a scope, terms, and approach tailored to your objectives before any work begins.
